Spaghetti

Here is our delicious Spaghetti Dish

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Meat sauce
  • Pasta Noodles
  • Spices & Salt

Steps

  1. Fill a pot halfway with water and boil
  2. You can turn down the heat once boiled and add pasta. Cook for 8 minutes stirring occasionally
  3. Once cooked, strain pasta and set aside
  4. In a pan, add some oil and your ground beef
  5. As the ground beef starts to cook, add your spices and salt
  6. Using a spatula, break up your ground beef and mix the spices around
  7. Once cooked, add your pasta and meat sauce in
  8. Mix around all the contents
